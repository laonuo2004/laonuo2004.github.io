Hello! I'm Yilong Zuo, a third-year undergraduate in Computer Science at [Beijing Institute of Technology](https://www.bit.edu.cn/), working with [Prof. Ronghua Li](https://ronghuali.github.io/).

## Research Interests

I'm interested in how graph structures and multimodal information interact, particularly at the intersection of multimodal learning, graph learning, and large language models. I care about problems that are clearly defined and genuinely worth studying—I prefer starting from concrete questions and building methodologies from there.

Recent work includes multimodal attributed graphs, multimodal document understanding with graph-based retrieval, and exploring how LLM-based agents can optimize graph structures.
